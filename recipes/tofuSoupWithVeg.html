Chinese Tofu Soup with Veggies

Description

This tofu soup is quite substantial as it includes both protein and vegetables

Ingredients

  • Soft tofu
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Medium Tomato
  • Bok Choy

Steps

  1. Soak dried ingredients
  2. Cut tofu, slice tomatoes into wedges, separate the stems and leaves of bok choy
  3. Heat cooking oil in wok
  4. Slowly pour in the water in which the mushrooms are soaked and bring to a boil
